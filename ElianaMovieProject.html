Eliana's Fav Sefarim This Year!

Here are 3 sefarim that I have learned from this year which helped me grow.

Mesilas Yesharim

A must read for any Jew aspiring to understand life.

Beis Halevi on Bitachon

This book will completely transform how you view hardships in your life. It unlocks a new mindset of how we view Hashem in our lives.

Spare the Child

This book is such an incredible work that will help parents approach parenting in a new way.
